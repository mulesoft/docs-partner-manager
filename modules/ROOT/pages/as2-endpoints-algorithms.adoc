= AS2 Signature and Encryption Algorithms

AS2 endpoints deployed from Partner Manager support the following signature and encryption algorithms:

* Signature algorithms
** `UNSIGNED (Default)`
** `MD5`
** `SHA1`
** `SHA224`
** `SHA256`
** `SHA384`
** `SHA512`
* Encryption algorithms
** `UNENCRYPTED (Default)`
** `AES128_CBC`
** `AES128_CCM`
** `AES128_GCM`
** `AES192_CBC`
** `AES192_CCM`
** `AES192_GCM`
** `AES256_CBC`
** `AES256_CCM`
** `AES256_GCM`
** `AES256_WRAP`
** `CAST5`
** `DES_EDE3`
** `RC2`
