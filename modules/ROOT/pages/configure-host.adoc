Your organization is considered the host partner in Anypoint Partner Manager.

Within host page you can:

** Add B2B identifiers.
** Add certificates your organization owns.
** Configure global validation and acknowledgement settings.
** Configure the storage location for transmission payloads.

== To configure the host:
. From within your Partner Manager environment, select name of your organization from the left-hand navigation.
. Click the *New Partner* button and fill in the receiving endpoint.

From here you can refer to the Reference section for detailed information on the various settings.
When you are ready, you can move on to configuring your partner.
