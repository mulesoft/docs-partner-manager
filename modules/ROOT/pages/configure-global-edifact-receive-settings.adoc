= Configuring Global EDIFACT Receive Settings

When EDIFACT messages received from your trading partners do not contain a UNA segment, Partner Manager uses the delimiters specified in the *Global EDIFACT settings: Receive from partners* window to parse the received EDIFACT payloads.
