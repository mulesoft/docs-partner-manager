Anypoint Partner Manager includes these types of endpoints.

From the Host side:

* Endpoints to receive transmissions from your partners.
* Endpoints to route messages from your partners to a target at your backend systems.
* Endpoints to send messages from your backend systems to your partners.

From the Partner side:

* Endpoints to send transmissions to your partners, including acknowledgments.

== Endpoint Name, Protocol and Type

[%header,cols="3s,7a"]
|===
|Setting |Description

|Name

|Name for this endpoint

Required:: Yes

Default:: Auto-populates based on the following values:

* *Protocol*
* *Type*


|Protocol

|Protocol for this endpoint

Required?::
No

Valid values::

* `AS2`
* `SFTP`

Default::

None

|Type

|Type of endpoint

Required?::

No

Valid values::

* `SEND`
* `RECEIVE`

Default::

None

|Default for partner_ checkbox

|Select this box if you want the choices you make for this endpoint to apply to all endpoints for this partner.

|===
